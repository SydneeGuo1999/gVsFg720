What should I do?

This is a test to study image display.
You will be shown pairs of images with different display conditions and will be asked to pick the one you like best; there are no other specific criteria.

Please enter your name to start the test.

Each pair of images will appear for 1 second.
Choose the image you like best by pressing '←' or '→' for the left or right image.
You choice will be indicated by a gray box in the position of the image you picked.
There is no time limit for making your decision.
You may change your selection before you move to the next pair.
Press '↓' to see next pair.

When the test has ended, an 'END' button will appaer.
Please click it to finish the test.
